How the Cleaning Check System Works

Our inspection system ensures dorms remain clean and comfortable through regular checks. Residents must be present during scheduled inspections, or all occupants will fail. A provided cleaning checklist outlines required tasks; uncompleted chores result in a fail and a reinspection three days later. Residents can hire professional cleaners at $35 per half hour to prepare for or recover from inspections. Semester- or year-long cleaning subscriptions are available to bypass inspections entirely. Missed cleaner appointments incur fines, and failed inspections cost $50 per resident, increasing to $100 after a second failure, with mandatory professional cleaning at $30 per half hour. Stay informed by checking schedules and completing chores on time!

If you'd like a bit more detail on how it all works, here's a rundown:

  1. Scheduled Inspections
    • We will post specific times on our website indicating when our inspectors will visit your dorm.
    • It is essential that someone from your apartment or dorm is present during the inspection. If no one is home when the inspector arrives, each inhabitant of the apartment will automatically receive a fail.
  2. Chore List and Cleaning Standards
    • A cleaning checklist will be provided ahead of time, outlining the specific chores that need to be completed.
    • If the tasks on the list are not completed or are insufficiently cleaned, the responsible resident(s) will receive a fail.
    • After a failed inspection, a reinspection will take place three days later.
  3. Hiring a Cleaner
    • If you fail the initial inspection, you have the option to hire a cleaner to take care of the missed chores before the reinspection.
    • Cleaners are available at a rate of $35 per half hour of cleaning.
    • You may also preemptively hire a cleaner to guarantee a pass on your cleaning check. This ensures your space meets the cleanliness standards without you having to perform the cleaning yourself.
  4. Subscription Options
    • For those who prefer not to deal with the monthly cleaning checks, we offer semester-long or year-long subscriptions to monthly professional cleanings.
    • By subscribing, youâ€™ll have peace of mind knowing that your space will be professionally cleaned on a regular basis, eliminating the need for inspections.
  5. Resident Responsibilities for Cleaner Appointments
    • If you request a cleaning, it is your responsibility to be present at the scheduled time to allow the cleaner to enter.
    • If the cleaner arrives and no one is home, you will be fined for failure to allow entry.
  6. Fines and Charges for Failed Inspections
    • The cost of failing an inspection is $50 per resident.
    • If a resident fails the second inspection, the fine increases to $100 per resident.
    • After a second failed check, we will automatically schedule a professional cleaning, for which you will be charged $30 per half hour of cleaning.
