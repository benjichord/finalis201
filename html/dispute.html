Fail Your Initial Inspection?

Follow the steps below to dispute your check and resolve the issue.

Dispute Submission Rules

  • Disputes MUST be sent in before 11:59 PM on the day of the initial inspection.
  • All disputes sent after 11:59 PM will be ignored.

What to Include in Your Dispute

  • Submit time-stamped photo evidence and a video showing the areas in question.
  • Email all dispute materials to: readycheckcleaning@gmail.com
  • We will compare your submitted evidence with our footage and photos of your unit.

Resolution Process

  • If your dispute is approved, we will notify you via email.
  • If your dispute is rejected, we will share our evidence and reasoning with you.

If you feel there’s been a mistake, don’t hesitate to reach out! We’re committed to ensuring fairness and transparency in all cleaning inspections.
